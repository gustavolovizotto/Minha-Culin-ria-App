Ingredients

Yields: 1 Loaf

Steps

01

Autolyse

In a large bowl, mix the bread flour, whole wheat flour, and 350g of water until a shaggy dough forms. Cover with a damp towel and let it rest for 1 hour.

timer 1:00:00
02

Incorporate Starter & Salt

Add the active starter and salt to the dough. Dimple them in with your fingers, then perform a series of slap and folds for about 5 minutes until the dough becomes smoother and cohesive.

03

Bulk Fermentation (Stretch & Folds)

Transfer to a clean container. Over the next 4 hours, perform 4 sets of stretch and folds, spaced 45 minutes apart. The dough should become bubbly and increase in volume by 30-50%.
